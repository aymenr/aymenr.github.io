SABAQ

SABAQ (lesson) is an educational technology company that makes applications, content and tools to aid learning in and outside the classroom

I worked on MUSE- their flagship Android application. It is a digital library of lessons, interactive exercises and games for Math, Urdu, Science & English for primary school children.

What did I do?
  • Led a team of 3 designers coordinating and launching multiple UX, UI & illustration projects, and creating design systems for existing products 
  • Analyzed usage data to identify potential improvements, and used that to inform a redesign of the flagship MUSE application. Resulted in a statistically significant increase in conversion, retention & time-spent metrics 
  • Conducted design research and created wireframes, high-fidelity mocks, and prototypes for a new application section for kindergarteners
